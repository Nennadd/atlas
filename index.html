About Us

We are family owned business, manufacturing garment for some of the most respectable world fashion brands, since 2001. Our team consist of highly skilled craftsmen, some of them with more then 30 years of experience in various apparel production phases. We are dedicated to quality, precision and flexibility

These qualities qualified us for production of clothing for some of the world's most demanding fashion markets (France, German, Italy). Depending on your projects requirements, we are able to provide cut/sew as well as full package services. We welcome established brands, as well as a new fashion startups.

From small to large scale production - we give flexibility to the brands. to turn their creative ideas into a finished product. Working with the Atlas means working with the reliable, professional and fexible clothing production partner in Serbia
